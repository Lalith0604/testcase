Dr. M S Krishnakanth

Dr. M S Krishnakanth is a Veterinary Physician in Banjara Hills, Hyderabad and has an experience of 21 years in this field. Dr. M S Krishnakanth practices at Claws & Paws in Banjara Hills, Hyderabad and Claws and Paws Pet Clinic in Madhapur, Hyderabad. He completed Bachelor in Veterinary Science and Animal Husbandary (BVSc & AH) from College of Veterinary Science, Rajendranagar, SVVU in 2004. He is a member of FSPAI and Pet Practitioners Association of Twin Cities. Some of the services provided by the doctor are: Services for Birds,Treatments for Birds,Veterinary Dental Prophylaxis,Veterinary Chiropractic and Neurosurgery in Dogs and Cats etc.
